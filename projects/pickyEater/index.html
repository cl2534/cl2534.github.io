Picky Eater App

Have you ever had a struggle in choosing what to eat within your groups of people? No Worries! This app is here to help you and your group to make the "right" choice. and will generate a tournament game for you. (like a world cup tournament!)

Out of 15 cuisine selections, your first selection of cuisines will generate a "cuisine tournament game" for you (like a world cup tournament). The last cuisine to stand wins!

Lastly, based on your last cuisine and your location (which you mentioned on "Sign Up" page), the app will generate 5 closest restaurants near you. Click one of the restaurants will lead you to Yelp Page for more information!
